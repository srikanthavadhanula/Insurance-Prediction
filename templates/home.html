Insurance Predictor

This website helps in predicting your insurance charges based on your info

How To Use

By filling some of your details like age, body mass index [BMI], gender , children and few other details, We will be predicting your insurance charges

How Does It Work

We took the insurance dataset from kaggle and did data preprocessing and identified the correlation between the varaibles by using some visualization tools like seaborn and matplotlib Based on the data, we have built two models those are Random Forest Regressor and Linear Regression What ever data you have provided, it will the predicted from that model
